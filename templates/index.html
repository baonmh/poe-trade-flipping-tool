POE Trade Flipping

Local tool for Path of Exile 1 & 2: rates, flips, crafting ideas, and convert tricks. Data comes from poe.ninja public APIs — not affiliated with Grinding Gear Games.

Prices are indicative (listing snapshots); live trade prices differ. No game or trade-site login is used.

Use the header to pick POE1 / POE2 and league. Open Settings for profit thresholds and refresh intervals. Each tab has its own Refresh to load data.
